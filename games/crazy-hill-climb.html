How to Play

Controls:

  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • Left/Right Arrows: Adjust vehicle balance
  • Spacebar: Special ability (if available)

Game Objectives

Complete the track within the time limit, collect coins along the way, and maintain your vehicle's balance. Be careful not to flip over, as this will result in game over. Complete various challenges to unlock new characters and tracks.

Game Tips

  • Accelerate in time when going uphill
  • Control your speed when going downhill to avoid flipping over
  • Adjust your vehicle's balance quickly when encountering obstacles
  • Be sure to collect coins along the way to unlock new content
  • Different animal characters may have unique special abilities - use them wisely
