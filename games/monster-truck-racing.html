How to Play

Controls:

  • Right Arrow: Accelerate
  • Left Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Down Arrow: Adjust Balance

Game Objectives

Race your monster truck through challenging obstacle courses while performing gravity-defying stunts. Compete against ruthless AI opponents and aim for victory in each race. Master the physics-based mechanics to pull off impressive jumps and flips while maintaining control. Progress through the game by unlocking new trucks and tracks as you dominate each challenge.

Game Tips

  • Perfect your mid-air control for both spectacular stunts and safe landings
  • Use the balance controls to prevent your truck from flipping over
  • Practice timing your jumps to maximize distance and height
  • Learn each track's layout to anticipate upcoming obstacles
  • Sometimes a controlled crash is better than trying to save an impossible landing
  • Unlock stronger trucks to handle more challenging tracks
  • For especially difficult jumps, maintain a steady speed rather than going full throttle
  • Try to complete Track 5 without flipping for an extra challenge
