Game Features

  • 30 Brutal Challenge Levels - Progressive difficulty from beginner to extreme
  • Realistic Physics Engine - Authentic vehicle handling and crash physics
  • Truck Customization - Personalize and upgrade your monster truck
  • Slow-Motion Crashes - Cinematic effects for spectacular failures
  • Mid-Air Controls - Adjust your vehicle while airborne for perfect landings
  • Vertical Cliff Climbs - Challenge extreme mountain terrain
  • Hidden Shortcuts - Secret paths throughout levels
  • Aerial Stunt System - Perform impressive jumps and tricks

How to Play

Controls:

  • D / Right Arrow: Accelerate
  • A / Left Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • W / Up Arrow: Jump
  • S / Down Arrow: Tilt Backward

Game Objectives

Navigate challenging mountain terrain with your monster truck, completing 30 progressively difficult levels. Conquer vertical cliff faces, perform aerial stunts, and discover hidden shortcuts. Master mid-air control to stick perfect landings and avoid disastrous crashes. Customize your truck to improve performance and handle increasingly challenging obstacles.

Game Tips

  • Perfect your mid-air control for successful landings
  • Use the tilt backward function to keep your wheels down when jumping
  • Look for hidden shortcuts to gain advantages in difficult levels
  • Approach vertical cliff faces with enough speed to make it to the top
  • Time your jumps carefully to clear obstacles
  • Manage your speed - sometimes slower is better for tricky terrain
  • Be especially careful on Level 15 with the spinning saw blades
  • Customize your truck to match your driving style and level requirements
